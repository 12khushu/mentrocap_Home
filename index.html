Our Goal

We help innovators and business leaders achieve commercial success to create exponential value for all stake holders.

HOW IT WORKS

MentorCap Management Private Limited is an investment firm focused on innovation scaling with the objective of generating exponential returns. We are a gestalt of deeply committed multi-disciplinary entrepreneurs, investors, and professionals, each with rich experience, a stellar track record, and a cohesive network, which are invaluable in pursuing our shared mission of scaling undervalued inventions and undiscovered intellectual potential for the greatest paradigm shifts in technology and scientific entrepreneurship.

WHO WE ARE?

MentorCap Management collaborates with innovators and business leaders by leveraging its multi-dimensional expertise in areas of investment & finance, technology, engineering & manufacturing, digitalization, business building & operations, organization development, and scale-up strategies. It engages with strategic partners and financial investors at different phases of the venture from early stage to growth stage. The processes run through propriety digital platforms that identify, nurse, operationalize and scale-up technologies speedily and efficiently.

With a fortitude for excellence, our core echelon has a commendable expertise in this domain and collectively have an impressive track record to reckon with. Meet our team…

WHAT WE DO?

Identify, validate, connect and augment ideas, technologies and businesses from across the globe that are focused on solving India’s high-impact problems, through a robust process of stage-gated screening and evaluation.

Select proven technologies and businesses, with large addressable markets; develop sound and winning business strategies; run business prototyping to minimize risk of failure and provide a solid basis for scale up plans.

Establish formidable business partnerships that help shape future technologies through robust business ecosystems.

Build scalable business ventures that make positive & sustainable environmental and societal impact, leveraging proprietary platforms, technology and network effects.

Partner with or own existing businesses to grow, unleash & realize their stretch potential through strong formation and implementation of business strategies covering product-market-operational efficiencies.

MENTORCAP PLATFORM

We bridge and integrate several sectors of small to mid-level enterprises with tech start-ups, giving them appropriate infrastructural and capital requirements, endowing them with competitive advantage and growth acceleration strategies.

This includes operational support, commercialization, shared manufacturing, etc. Our diversified portfolio is currently leveraged and propelled by our proprietary MentorCap platform.
